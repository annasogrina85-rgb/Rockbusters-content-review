Brand voice

How every Rockbusters post reads. Check it sounds like us.

Vibe: bold, punchy, insider, motivational, a little irreverent — the voice of a strong climber hyping you up at the crag. Confident, never corporate.

  • Person First-person "we" (the community) talking to "you" (the climber). "We'll push you as hard as you let us — you'll leave a better climber."
  • Casing Headlines are ALL-CAPS, short, declarative, often three-beat. Body copy is sentence case.
  • Punctuation Staccato. Periods as drumbeats: "CLIMB. HARD. EAT. SLEEP. CLIMB. AGAIN." Em-dashes & ellipses for momentum.
  • Climbing fluency Real lexicon as a badge — beta, send, redpoint, onsight, flash, crusher, tie in, DWS, trad, project. Don't over-explain it.
  • Numbers as flex "1 coach / 2–6 climbers" · "100% rock time" · "1% For The Planet".
  • Emoji None. Energy comes from typography and red, not emoji.

Visual identity — in words

The rules behind the look, in plain language.

Palette
Three colours do the work: signal red #e30613, hard black, clean white. Red is an accent and an attention weapon — one thing per view (CTA, a key stat), never a wash. A cool granite-grey ramp handles surfaces and secondary text.
Typography
One family — Montserrat — pushed to extremes. Display: 800–900, UPPERCASE, tight tracking (−0.02em), near-solid leading (0.95), words stacked into a block. Body: 400–600, sentence case. Eyebrows: bold uppercase, wide 0.14em tracking, red.
Backgrounds
Two registers: clean white/chalk for content, hard black sections for impact. Grungy halftone textures dropped in at low opacity. No smooth gradients, no blurry glows. Real climbing photography, full-bleed, with a dark protection gradient for legible type.
Layout
Confident, grid-driven, generous vertical rhythm. Strong left-aligned headline blocks; big type allowed to dominate. Edge-to-edge dark bands break the page. Max width 1200px.
Shape & depth
Hard-edged and athletic. Tight radii — cards 6px, feature cards 12px max; buttons, tags & badges are full pills. Crisp 1–2px borders (3px for the "stamp" look). Shadows neutral and restrained — depth comes from contrast and texture, not blur.
Motion
Quick and punchy, never floaty. 120–200ms, ease-out. Hover darkens red / lifts 1px; press shrinks slightly.
