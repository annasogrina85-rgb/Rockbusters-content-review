Surface container for trip cards, coach profiles, feature blocks.

```jsx
<Card tone="dark" interactive>
  <Eyebrow>Sport climbing</Eyebrow>
  <h3>Kalymnos road trip</h3>
</Card>
```

Tones: `light` (default, bordered white), `subtle` (chalk grey), `dark` (black — high impact), `red`. `interactive` adds hover-lift for clickable cards. `padded={false}` for cards with full-bleed images at the top. Radius is 12px; keep content left-aligned.
