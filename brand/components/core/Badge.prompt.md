Small uppercase badge for status, difficulty grades or trip categories.

```jsx
<Badge variant="red">Women-only</Badge>
<Badge variant="black" shape="square">8a+</Badge>
```

Variants: `red`, `black`, `outline`, `light`. Shapes: `pill` (default), `square` (the "stamp" look — good for grades). Keep text to 1–2 short words, uppercase handled automatically.
